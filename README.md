# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, category-wise and region-wise performance.

## Tools Used
- MS Excel

## Dataset
Sample sales dataset with 25 records including Order Date, Product, Category, Region, Quantity, Price, and Sales.

## Analysis Steps
- Data cleaning and formatting
- Sales calculation (Quantity Ã— Price)
- Total revenue calculation
- Product-wise sales analysis
- Category-wise sales analysis
- Region-wise sales analysis
- Monthly sales trend analysis using pivot tables

## Key Insights
- Laptops generate the highest revenue among all products.
- Electronics category contributes the maximum share of total sales.
- South region is the top-performing region.
- Accessories sell in higher quantity but generate lower revenue.
- Sales show a stable trend during the month.

## Recommendations
- Increase focus on high-revenue products like laptops.
- Improve marketing strategies in high-performing regions.
- Bundle accessories with main products to increase revenue.
